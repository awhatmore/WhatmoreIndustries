Overview

Dynamic ThermaGrip uses regulated heat transfer to maintain optimal tire temperature, extending peak grip across variable race conditions. Instead of passive tire heating pre-race, this system reclaims engine heat and channels it through custom thermally conductive paths to tires in real-time.

Engineering Goals

  • Maintain tire temp in optimal grip range
  • Reduce cold-lap underperformance
  • Extend compound life with thermal balancing

Use Case

For racing teams, military transport in extreme cold, and robotics requiring high-friction terrain adherence.
