Calculation details

Enter values and press Calculate to see step-by-step math.

Formula used per-hit (effective damage): effective = standard - (durablePct/100) * (standard - durable)

Time-to-kill calculation (sustained, includes reloads): fireRate = RPM / 60  (rounds per second) firingTime = shots / fireRate reloads = floor((shots - 1) / mag) totalReloadTime = reloads * reloadTime TTK = firingTime + totalReloadTime

Notes

This calculator assumes continuous fire at the stated RPM with full magazines and instantaneous first shot; it does not model spread, accuracy, headshot multipliers, damage falloff, or interrupted firing. Reloads happen only when magazine is exhausted. If effective damage ≤ 0 the weapon cannot damage the target under this model.
Tip: adjust RPM or magazine size to model belt-fed weapons vs small magazines. All times are in seconds.
