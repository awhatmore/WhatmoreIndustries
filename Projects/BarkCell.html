Overview

BarkCell Battery is a novel battery architecture using tree bark-derived carbon frameworks, engineered under high pressure and aligned via algorithmic field harmonics. By treating wood-like materials in pressure chambers or deep-ocean pressure, we can create carbon frameworks with high density and conductivity, ideal for energy storage.

Deep-Sea Variant

Exploits natural oceanic pressure to create carbon alignments without industrial chambers. BarkCell units can be suspended in custom capsules at depth, then retrieved and processed into energy-dense modules.

Advantages

  • Bio-derived material
  • Low environmental impact
  • Enhanced density under pressure synthesis

Use Cases

  • Off-grid energy systems
  • Extreme environmental batteries (submarine drones, etc.)
