Ingenuity Through Subconscious Innovation

"Where ITSI is the engine, this is the drive."

Overview

This capsule expands on ITSI, describing how to allow ingenuity to flow *through* subconscious programming rather than simply emerging from it. It’s about trusting not just the source of your creative process, but the continuous channel through which you act.

Method

  • Recognize subconscious hesitation as an old protection layer
  • Design your space, language, and schedule to allow subconscious flow
  • Act on ideas in real-time — bypass delay by trusting the "through path"

Distinctions

  • ITSI: The art of drawing ideas from within
  • Ingenuity Through: The skill of letting them move into action uninterrupted
