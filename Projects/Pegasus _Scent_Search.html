System Overview

2. Detection & Search Agents

  • Drones & Robots: Equipped with miniaturized gas chromatographs and electronic noses (e-noses).
  • Real-Time Pattern Matching: Constantly compares airborne VOCs against stored profiles.
  • Dynamic Search Algorithms: Adjusts drone paths based on wind, topography, thermal activities.

3. Pegasus Core Integration

  • Subconscious Pattern Prediction: Estimates likely movement paths based on victim psychology.
  • Multi-Agent Orchestration: Coordinates K9 units, humans, drones, and robotics into intelligent mesh.
  • Environmental Modeling: Models wind shear, convection currents, chemical interference.

4. Situational Adaptability

  • Disaster-Aware Modes: Custom search behaviors for fires, floods, avalanches, earthquakes.
  • Real-Time Environmental Feedback Loop: Adapts strategies dynamically.
