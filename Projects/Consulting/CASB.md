Not for roofers.

---

## Core Model (lock this in)

> Observe → Extract → Map → Systemize → Hand Off

This becomes your signature workflow.

---

## 1. [OBSERVE] — Controlled Field Intake (low energy)

**Your rules:**
- 3–5 interactions max  
- No selling  
- No convincing  

**You’re extracting:**
- How they currently get clients  
- What they hate about it  
- What triggers them to act  

> Think of it like: reverse-engineering their decision brain

---

## 2. [EXTRACT] — Turn chaos into signals

After those conversations, write:

- Top 3 pain patterns  
- Top 3 trigger phrases  
- Top 3 existing behaviors  

**Example:**
- “Leads are inconsistent”  
- “I rely on referrals”  
- “I don’t trust marketing agencies”  

> This becomes your targeting DNA

---

## 3. [MAP] — Where do these people exist at scale?

Ask:

> “If I needed 100 of these exact people, where would I go?”

Build a simple map:
- Platforms (Facebook, Yelp, Google Maps, directories)  
- Geographic clusters  
- Business size/type  

You are NOT contacting yet.

> You are building the terrain model

---

## 4. [SYSTEMIZE] — Build the machine

Convert everything into a system:

- Lead list generation (scraping, filtering, organizing)  
- Categorization (high value vs low value)  
- Messaging logic (based on triggers you found)  

Even if you don’t automate yet, structure it so it *can* be automated later.

That’s key.

---

## 5. [HAND OFF] — You don’t run it

This is where most people mess up.

**You do NOT become:**
- The outreach person  
- The closer  
- The operator  

**You hand off:**
- Lead lists  
- Targeting logic  
- Messaging structure  

To:
- Your friend  
- Or whoever is doing sales  

> You stay the system architect
