Aegis Mesh

Autonomous Aerial Protection Layer for Distributed Force Defense

Problem

Small aerial threats create a last-mile protection gap (100–1000 m) for squads, light units, and forward bases.

Concept

Self-organizing aerial coverage layer that dynamically allocates protection and reports coverage state and overstretch.

Core Behavior

  • Fills gaps automatically
  • Consolidates near friendly units
  • Expands forward when stable
  • Contracts under pressure

Zone State Model

  • Green — stable
  • Yellow — strained
  • Red — insufficient
  • Black — breakdown imminent

Operator Output

  • Coverage State
  • Screen Integrity (%)
  • Overstretch Direction

Outcome

  • Reduced threat penetration
  • Increased reaction time
  • Clear visibility of protection limits
