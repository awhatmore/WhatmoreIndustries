Apollo Atmospheric Space Suit

The Apollo Atmospheric Space Suit is a modular suit design inspired by NASA's mechanical counterpressure (MCP) research, optimized for mobility, dexterity, and safety under high internal pressures for use in extraterrestrial and extreme terrestrial environments.

Core Innovations

Multi-Pressure Zone Architecture

  • Outer Armadillo Shell: Articulated scale system for mobility and pressure resistance.
  • Mid-Layer Structural Scaffold: Structural mesh for pressure isolation and deformation resistance.
  • Inner Pressure Containment Layer: Elastic high-pressure layer for comfortable conformity.

Advanced Dexterity Glove System

  • Rigid fingertips for precision grip.
  • Exo-tendons and spring joints for improved dexterity.
